Picked ginger

Peel the ginger

Wash it and cut it up in small strips

Bring water to the boil

Throw ginger in and let it boil for 2 minutes

Drain the ginger and let it cool

Add 120 ml of vinegar (regular + some balsamic) and 60g of sugar in a pot

Boil till sugar melts

Set aside to cool

Once both the giner and vinegar mixtures are cool, drain again the ginger (squeeze all water in it) and put it in a jar.

Pour the vinegar mixture over it and put it in the fridge for minim 12-24 hrs
